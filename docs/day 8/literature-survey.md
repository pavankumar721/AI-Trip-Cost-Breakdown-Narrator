# Literature Survey

## Introduction

Artificial Intelligence is widely used for content generation, customer communication, and automated assistance. This survey analyzes existing AI tools relevant to narrative generation and customer support.

---

## ChatGPT

Developer: OpenAI

Features:
- Conversational AI
- Content generation
- Summarization
- Question answering

Strengths:
- Excellent language quality
- Good instruction following
- Structured responses

Limitations:
- Not specialized for travel package narration
- Requires prompt customization

---

## Google Gemini

Developer: Google

Features:
- Multimodal AI
- Reasoning capabilities
- Information assistance

Strengths:
- Fast responses
- Good contextual understanding

Limitations:
- Generic outputs for travel cost explanations
- No predefined narration workflow

---

## Claude

Developer: Anthropic

Features:
- Natural language generation
- Long-form content creation
- Safe conversational responses

Strengths:
- Professional writing style
- Detailed explanations

Limitations:
- Requires custom prompt design
- Not travel-domain specific

---

## Observation

Existing AI systems are general-purpose assistants. They generate explanations effectively but do not provide a standardized workflow for travel package cost narration.
